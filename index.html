About V-FIESTA 2020

VFiesta is a Global Entrepreneur Fest hosted by IEEE PIE KERALA SECTION. It is a platform to explore and experiment the Enterpreneurial skills, Managerial styles and Technical innovations.
V-Fiesta is the celebration to prove your mettle in your area of expertise, be it technical skills managerial prowess-A chance to explore many areas through a single event .

Online

V-FIESTA 2020 EVENTS

BIZCON

Mini Entrepreneurial Summit

Bizcon is packed full of interactive sessions to unlock every half an hour. We’ve got 4 talk sessions where entrepreneurs from across the world convene to share their tale of success. Dig deep into a world where you are the boss.




SWIPE UP!

Digital Marketing Workshop.

Digital marketing is the present buzzword in the world of entrepreneurship. We as a part of V-Fiesta organise a 5 day workshop series to realise the real potential of Digital marketing and cultivate yourselves into real time experts.




SUITS

Best Manager Competition.

Event is intended to findout the Handy Andy who turns out the worst to the best. It's structured with multiple rounds including a surprise round. The Managerial style of the person in its every perspective is reckoned for him /her entitled with "SUITS"




INNOVATHON

Global Virtual Hackathon.

It is the most creative and interesting event of V-Fiesta 2020 where the team members are given with various problem statements on Rural Development. A team of atmost 5 people collaborate and come up with an innovative solution.




OPEN MIC

A space where interested participants gets a chance to showcase their talents.

FAQ

1. What is V- Fiesta?

V - Fiesta is a Global Entrepreneur Fest to celebrate the Entrepreneurship culture, Managerial Styles and Technological Innovations. It is structured with:
i. BIZCON - Global Summit.
ii. SWIPE UP! - Workshop on Digital Media and Content Marketing.
iii. INNOVATHON - Global Virtual Hackathon.
iv. SUITS - Best manager
v. OPEN MIC

2. Is there any fee to participate?

Yes. There will be a registration fee of Rs.150 for which you can particpate for Hackathon or the combo of 5 events Bizcon, Swipe Up, Suits, Innovathon & Open Mic.

3.Guidelines for registration.

If all team members have registered for VFiesta (Five In One ), then no more registration is required for hackathon.
If you have registered for hackathon as a team, you can only attend the same.

4. Can I cancel my registration?

The registration cannot be cancelled once the payment is done. And no refunds will be initiated.
