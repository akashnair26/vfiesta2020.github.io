About V-FIESTA 2020

V-Fiesta is a Global Entrepreneur fest hosted by IEEE PIE Kerala Section, as a platform to explore and experiment the Entrepreneurial skills, Managerial styles and Technical Innovations.
It is a celebration orate filled with technical and managerial events wherein the perspective through which we have looked is varied.

WHERE
Online

WHEN
November 7 to 22, 2020

V-FIESTA 2020 Events

BIZCON

Mini Entrepreneurial Summit

Bizcon is packed full of interactive sessions to unlock every half an hour. We’ve got 4 talk sessions where entrepreneurs from across the world convene to share their tale of success. Dig deep into a world where you are the boss.




SWIPE UP!

Digital Marketing Workshop.

A 5-day workshop series in which the digital marketing strategies will be discussed. As Marketing being the prominent word for entrepreneurship, we had included the tricks and hacks to excel.




SUITS

Best Manager Competition.

Event is intended to findout the Handy Andy who turns out the worst to the best. It's structured with multiple rounds including a surprise round. The Managerial style of the person in its every perspective is reckoned for him /her entitled with "SUITS"
